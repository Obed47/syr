Upcoming events and announcements

Reminders and Announcements to new retreaters

  1. This year we have our usual games
    • Offhand hynm singing
    • Bible Quiz
    • Fast Bible opening
    • Solo singing
  2. We shall have our usual 2 days fasting
    • Wednesday the 12 august 2023
    • Saturday the 15 august 2023
  3. People shall be stoped from moving in and out of the retreat premisses inorder for us to really retreat
  4. All retreaters must sleep at the retreat ground even those from the Buea environs
  5. Everyone is reminded to bring their sleeping material and warm clothing. Buea is cold
  6. The retreat shall hold at the Buea Baird memorial High school at Bonduma Tap

What's Coming?

  1. The young Leaders forum at Douala Cite de la Paix
    • This seminar is aimed at training young baptised christian brothers to lead in the church be it in Singing, Preaching, Exhorting, Serving or any activity in the church so that we can we assured of the future of the church and leave it in good hands.
  2. The Anual youth forum
    • This is another opportunity for youth from all over the nation to gather and strenghen themselves spiritually. This shall be held in a place that will be communicated to you latter on in the year in this platform. Get ready
